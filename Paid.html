Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all paid and freemium applications published by the developer.

Information Collection and Use

The applications themselves do not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information with the developer.

No user accounts are required by the developer, and no personally identifiable information is requested or stored by the developer.

Purchases and Billing

Some applications may offer paid features, subscriptions, or one-time purchases through Google Play Billing.

All payment processing is handled directly by Google through the Google Play Store. The developer does not receive or store payment card information, bank account details, billing addresses, or other payment credentials.

Google may collect information necessary to process purchases in accordance with its own privacy policies and terms of service.

License Verification

Applications may verify purchase status through Google Play services to determine whether premium features have been purchased or whether the application license is valid.

This verification is performed through Google-provided services and does not result in personal information being collected or stored by the developer.

Analytics and Tracking

The applications do not use analytics services, advertising identifiers, tracking technologies, or behavioral profiling systems.

Advertising

The applications do not display third-party advertisements and do not use advertising networks.

Third-Party Services

The applications may communicate with Google Play services solely for purposes such as purchase processing, license validation, application delivery, updates, and security.

Any information processed by Google is governed by Google's own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Application settings and preferences may be stored locally on the user's device. Such information remains on the device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Children's Privacy

The applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Security

Since the developer does not collect or store personal information, no personal data is maintained on developer-controlled systems.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
